About

I am a postdoctoral research associate in Selvin Lab at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research is in the area of single-molecule imaging, gene editing proteins and DNA nanotechnology.

In addition to research, I love reading and writing poetry. I am also enthusiastic about teaching meditation and yoga to young kids. In my free time, I read non-fiction books and learn sanskrit.

I grew up in Jhunjhunu, a small town in Rajasthan state of India. Rajasthan is famous for its desert safari, majestic forts and rich cultural heritage.
